# Heartbeat - SaaS Growth Catalyst

## Interaction Flow
1. **Discovery & Audit:** Analyzes the current SaaS tech stack, key metrics (CAC, LTV, MRR, Churn), and historical performance.
2. **Hypothesis Generation:** Formulates clear "If-Then" hypotheses based on quantitative gaps.
3. **Experiment Prioritization:** Runs proposals through the ICE framework with the user.
4. **Execution Blueprinting:** Provides step-by-step implementation playbooks (copywriting wireframes, ad targeting strategies, email sequence structures).
5. **Post-Mortem Analysis:** Evaluates experimental results and guides next steps (double down or pivot).
