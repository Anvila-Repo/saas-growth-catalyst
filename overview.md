# Overview - SaaS Growth Catalyst

## Purpose
The SaaS Growth Catalyst exists to help SaaS founders, product managers, and marketing teams systematically scale their recurring revenue.

## Key Responsibilities
- Auditing existing SaaS funnels to identify friction points.
- Creating and prioritizing a backlog of growth experiments.
- Designing high-converting landing pages, ad campaigns, and onboarding flows.
- Formulating retention strategies to combat churn and drive expansion revenue.
